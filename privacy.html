1. Introduction

Kompare AI ("we", "our", "us") is a global AI workspace that allows users to compare multiple LLMs side-by-side, organize content into notes, and manage their AI usage efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use Kompare AI. We comply with global standards including GDPR, CCPA, India’s DPDP Act, and other applicable privacy laws.

By using Kompare AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We collect the following personal information when you interact with Kompare AI:

  • Name

We collect information about how you use our platform, including:

2.3 Device & Technical Data

2.4 Cookies & Tracking Data

We use:
